The following programs were written using p5.js

  • PHEN 100

  • Pixel Coordinate System - This demonstrates the pixel coordinate system: the location of the origin and the direction of the axes. This also demonstrates the use of Processing's size function and Processing's mouseX and mouseY variables.
  • World Coordinate System 2 - This demonstrates my world coordinate system: the location of Processing's display window in the world and the direction of the axes. This also demonstrates the use of my setDisplayXInWorld, setDisplayYInWorld, setDisplayWidthInWorld and setDisplayHeightInWorld functions and my mouseXInWorld and mouseYInWorld variables.
